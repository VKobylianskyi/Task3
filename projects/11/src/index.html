Ingredients & Extracts

Amazonian Cacay Nut Oil
Amazonian Cacay Nut Oil has 3 times more vitamin A (Retinol) than Rosehip Oil, and higher levels of vitamin E and F than Argan Oil and is known to visibly improve skin tone and reduce the appearance of environmental damage.
Found In: Fresh Start, Power Detox, Balance Beam, Soft Touch, Glow Remedy, Zero Gravity, Beauty Sleep
Apple Fruit Extract
Apple Fruit Extract delivers a powerful punch of vitamin C and natural acids to help brighten the appearance of skin.
Found in: Balance Beam, Soft Touch, Acne Edit Toner, Acne Edit Overnight Treatment
Apricot Kernel Extract
Apricot Kernel Extract is rich in vitamins A & C, and is known for its anti-inflammatory properties, that help ease sensitive skin, while softening and brightening the overall appearance of skin tone.
Found in: Power Detox & Soft Touch
Bamboo Charcoal
Bamboo Charcoal draws out impurities and unclogs pores, removing toxins, dirt and dead skin cells, leaving your skin refreshingly smooth.
Found in: Glow Remedy
Blueberry Extract
Blueberry Extract is rich in natural antioxidants known to help repair sun damage and increase cell turnover.
Found in: Glow Remedy
Chia Seed Extract
Chia Seed Extract contains the perfect 3-to-1 ratio of omega-3 and omega-6 essential fatty acids for optimal skin nourishment. It helps to combat the visible signs of aging, while making the skin look radiant.
Found in: Beauty Sleep
Cinnamon Bark
Cinnamon Bark is known to help clarify and balance the look of skin tone by bringing blood to the skin surface and drying up excess oil.
Found in: Fresh Start
Cocoa Seed Butter
Cocoa Seed Butter helps fight environmental free radicals and acts as a barrier to maintain optimal moisture levels of the skin.
Found in: Beauty Sleep
Coconut Oil
Coconut Oil has natural anti-bacterial and anti-fungal properties known to penetrate deep within the skin to provide serious hydration without clogging pores.
Found in: Soft Touch, Acne Edit Spot Treatment,
Acne Edit Overnight Treatment
Coffee Extract
Coffee Extract helps to firm and tone the look of skin.
Found in: Soft Touch
Fermented Red Ginseng
Fermented Red Ginseng is extremely rich in phytonutrients to help firm skin tone and brighten the overall appearance of skin.
Found in: Power Detox
Ginger
Ginger contains over 40 antioxidants known to protect against free radical damage and help even the appearance of tone and texture. Also known for its soothing, anti-inflammatory properties, Ginger eases the skin, while reducing the look of redness and irritation.
Found in: Fresh Start, Acne Edit Spot Treatment
Glycolic Acid
Glycolic Acid is a powerful alpha hydroxy acid (AHA) and a holy grail for exfoliation. Glycolic Acid effectively removes the outermost layer of dead cells, revealing a brighter, fresher-looking complexion.
Found in: Acne Edit Spot Treatment, Acne Edit Overnight Treatment
Gotu Kola
Gotu Kola packs a potent combination of amino acids, beta carotene, fatty acids and phytochemicals known to help fight the visible signs of aging and protect skin from environmental stressors.
Found in: Acne Edit Cleanser, Acne Edit Toner, Acne Edit Spot Treatment, Acne Edit Overnight Treatment
Grape Seed Oil
Grape Seed Oil contains natural anti-bacterial and anti-fungal properties that penetrate deep within the skin to provide serious hydration without clogging pores – making this a hero ingredient for acne prone skin. With high levels of linoleic acid, Grape Seed Oil helps the vitamin E and vitamin C in your skin to be more efficient.
Found In: Fresh Start, Power Detox, Balance Beam, Flash Matte, Soft Touch, Glow Remedy, Zero Gravity, Beauty Sleep
